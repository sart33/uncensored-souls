# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2026

Uncensored Souls is a local-first application.

- All chats, characters, settings, API keys and generated images are stored only on your device.
- No personal data is collected, stored or transmitted by the developer.
- The only external data transfers occur when you send API requests:
  - Chat requests → DeepSeek
  - Image generation requests → Novita AI

- You provide your own API keys.
- The developer has no access to your API keys or your content.

- No accounts are required.
- No analytics, tracking or telemetry is used.
- No personal profiles or user databases are maintained.

Optional donations may use external payment providers or cryptocurrency networks. The developer does not collect or store personal identity information through these payment methods.
