# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

Uncensored Souls is a fully local application.

- All chats, characters, settings, API keys and generated images are stored **only on your device**.
- No personal data is collected, stored or transmitted by the developer.
- The only external data transfer occurs when you send API requests:
  - Chat messages → DeepSeek
  - Image generation → Navita AI
- You provide your own API keys. The developer has no access to them or to your content.
- No analytics, tracking or telemetry is used.
